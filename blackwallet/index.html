Common Questions

Why does the bank block online income transfers?
Kenyan banks are required to verify the source of funds under CBK (Central Bank of Kenya) regulations. When they receive income from online platforms they don't recognise, they flag it and request source-of-funds documentation — a process that can take weeks.
